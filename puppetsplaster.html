Puppets and Plaster

Puppets and plaster is a puppet show and related craft activity in plaster painting for kids and their families to enjoy in a friendly and safe environment.

Puppets and Plaster is on each weekend and mid-week for both public and private bookings.

Commencing Saturday, April 14, 2012.

To avoid disappointment go to our online store for all dates, times and entry passes.
Otherwise tickets at door when not booked out.

Parking is provided at rear of premises.

5a Beltana Road, Pialligo, ACT 2609
