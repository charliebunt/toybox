Music

Sean and Charlotte are members of the Australian Performing Rights Association and have now made available to the public a digital cd of their original music and story adaptation. Originally their soundtrack could be downloaded for free at mp3.com but after more than 8000 hits it was decided to put it up for sale to help fund the theatre. Below are samples from the soundtracks and those wishing to buy a cd can do so by going to the Toybox shop on here.
